About

I'm a graduate student at the University of Trieste completing my Master's in Data Science, with a background in cognitive psychology and psycholinguistics. Currently writing my thesis on NLP-based analysis of psychological discourse while freelancing on language-related projects.

Human language fascinates me, and I always wonder if we (so called humans) can ever manage to fully fathom (or model) the linguistic structures in our brains or the cognitive processes that lie behind it (it is fascinating already that we are using language to do that). in the meantime I am trying to study the methods and the theory whether in computational linguistics, psycholinguistics, mere semantics/logic, or philosophy of language, in a attempt to have better tools for such a mission. In my free time, I enjoy reading novels and rock climbing.
